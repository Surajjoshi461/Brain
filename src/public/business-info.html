Welcome to Cafeboard

Cafeboard is an online platform that helps cafes and restaurants manage their daily operations more efficiently. Our system includes tools for order tracking, menu management, customer engagement, and integration with WhatsApp for communication and order updates.

What We Do

  • Manage online and in-house orders in real time
  • Offer digital menus and order-taking solutions
  • Send WhatsApp order confirmations, updates, and promotions
  • Support customer queries through AI-powered chat

WhatsApp Usage

We use WhatsApp Business API to help cafes stay connected with their customers. This includes sending order updates, delivery notifications, and responding to customer inquiries.

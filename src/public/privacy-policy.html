Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Botwot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use our services and applications integrated with Meta platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp).

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic user profile details (e.g., name, phone number, username, user ID)

  • Metadata such as timestamps, message statuses (sent, delivered, read)

2. How We Use the Information

  • To enable core features such as messaging, automation, or AI response
  • To improve our services and personalize user experiences
  • To monitor system health and detect abuse or violations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only with:

  • Meta platforms as required for integration (e.g., via APIs or Webhooks)
  • Service providers acting on our behalf (e.g., cloud storage or analytics)
  • Law enforcement or regulators if required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for providing our services, complying with legal obligations, and resolving disputes.

5. Your Choices

  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • You may opt out of non-essential communication at any time

6. Security

We implement standard industry security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.
